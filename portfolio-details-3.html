Presentation Technical Doc

A department stock application is a software program that helps organizations manage and keep track of their inventory levels across different departments or locations. It includes features such as a centralized database, barcode scanning or RFID technology, and reporting tools. The application helps businesses optimize their inventory management processes, reduce waste and stockouts, and improve overall efficiency. It allows users to place orders, transfer items, and receive alerts when stock levels fall below a certain threshold.
